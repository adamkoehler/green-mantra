News

We strive to keep stakeholders informed of the latest news about GreenMantra’s technology, products, and efforts to further develop the circular economy of plastics.

If you are a member of the media interested in more information or want to arrange to speak with a company expert, please contact Mike Freeman at Freeman Communications, mike@freemancommunications.biz, 513-733-1800.

Closed Loop Fund to Invest up to $3 Million In GreenMantra™ Technologies

Date: July 26, 2017 by greenmantra

GreenMantra Technologies, a rapidly growing clean technology company that produces high-value polymer products from waste plastics, announced today that Closed Loop Fund will invest up to $3 million into an expansion of GreenMantra’s manufacturing operations in Brantford, Ontario.
