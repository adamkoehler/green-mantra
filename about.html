Our Story

GreenMantra Technologies is the world’s only company creating high-value waxes and the specialty chemicals from waste plastics. Our products add value as processing aids by improving throughput and reducing manufacturing costs, and as additives through enhancing the performance of finished products. By creating value from plastic waste, we are helping to drive a more circular economy where plastics are beneficially reused rather than landfilled.

Company Highlights

  • Founded in 2010
  • 5,000 metric ton annual manufacturing capacity (under expansion)
  • Based in Branford, Ontario
  • Highly efficient process: 1 lb. of plastic waste = .94 lb. of specialty wax
  • Producer of polyethylene and polypropylene waxes, modified polymers and specialty chemicals
  • 60 employees
  • Recipient of numerous industry awards, including 2016 R&D100 Gold Award for Green Technology
  • Privately held
