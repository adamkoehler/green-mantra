Asphalt Paving

CeranovusTM additives provide manufacturers of asphalt paving with enhanced processing, while reducing costs through lower energy requirements and fewer emissions.

In hot mix asphalt, our wax modifiers:

  • allow an increased percentage of RAP (reclaimed asphalt product) in the mix
  • improve asphalt flow
  • improve stiffness and prevent rutting
  • enhance processing and performance of formulations with recycled tire rubber

In warm mix asphalt, our waxes can:

  • lower required mixing and compaction temperatures 
  • enable longer transport distances
  • extend the construction season via workability in lower temperatures
