Job Description

Production Associate Tasks Include:

  • Assist Plant Operators with daily set up of machines and reactors for shift
  • Transport raw materials, finished product and equipment throughout plant manually or using powered equipment
  • Feed and unload production machinery and equipment
  • Check and weigh materials and products
  • Take delivery of materials from suppliers
  • Sort, pack, crate and package materials and products
  • Perform general duties such as basic construction, painting and other manual tasks
  • Clean work areas and equipment
  • Assist other workers to operate, repair and maintain equipment and tools
  • Participate in process improvement initiatives as required
  • Identify issues with production equipment and report immediately to Operations Manager
  • Perform other duties as assigned by overseer

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or GED
  • Must have experience in plastics and/or chemicals processing/production (industrial or commercial products, materials)
  • Technical / mechanical aptitude
  • Working knowledge of production equipment and understanding of manufacturing processes as it relates to plastic processing
  • Ability to use hand tools
  • Forklift experience / license
  • Must be able to stand for long periods of time
  • Must be able to lift up to 55 pounds
  • Demonstrated problem solving skills
  • Must be able to weigh a variety of decision alternatives and provide a recommendation based on sound analysis
  • Must be able to multitask and prioritize tasks with competing deadlines
  • Must be well organized and able to demonstrate a high degree of accuracy in all tasks
  • Must be able to clearly and concisely present information in verbal and written form
  • Can-do attitude with strong sense of urgency
  • Highly motivated, conscientious, self-managed team player, with experience working in a production team environment
  • Self-starter with the ability to work with a collaborative, driven team of skilled professionals
  • Safety conscience and professional
