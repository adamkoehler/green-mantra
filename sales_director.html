SALES DIRECTOR

GreenMantra™ Technologies is a specialty chemical company that has commercialized a patented and proprietary catalytic depolymerization technology to economically produce high-value industrial waxes, polymer additives, and other specialty chemicals derived from recycled plastic feedstocks using an innovative and environmentally friendly process. Through our proprietary thermo-catalytic system and patented process, we can cost-effectively convert waste plastics, including hard-to-recycle materials such as grocery bags and film, into high-value waxes and other specialty chemicals. We sell specialty wax products, marketed under the Ceranovus™ brand name, for use in a broad range of applications in polymer processing, adhesives, coatings, roofing and paving, and other industries. The company is at the forefront of providing an environmentally positive alternative source for synthetic waxes, reducing reliance on traditional fossil fuels as a raw material. This helps advance progress toward the goal of a circular economy that responsibly reuses waste streams.
