Creating Value From Waste Plastics

We are a clean technology leader that produces high-value synthetic polymer additives, waxes, and other chemicals from recycled waste plastics. We are the first company in the world to upcycle post-consumer plastics into synthetic polymers and other specialty materials. We are advancing a more circular economy for plastics where they are beneficially reused rather than thrown away.

Our Ceranovus™ synthetic waxes are used in:

  • asphalt
    roofing

  • Asphalt
    Paving

  • Polymer
    Processing

  • adhesives, inks
    and coatings

GreenMantra’s unique manufacturing process enables us to develop customized products to meet exact requirements and enable:

  • improved manufacturing throughput
  • reduced formulation costs
  • enhanced finished product performance

Growing to
Meet Demand

GreenMantra is growing rapidly and expanding our manufacturing facilities in Branford, Ontario to meet demand for specialty waxes. Additionally, we are building a production line featuring new technology that will transform waste polystyrene into modified polymers for inks and other applications.

Working Toward a
Cleaner Environment

Of the 300 million tons of plastic produced globally each year, only about 10 percent is recycled. We are committed to the cost-effective transformation of plastic waste, even hard to recycle materials such as plastic film and grocery bags, into valuable products – improving the environment by beneficially reusing spent materials. Wax is traditionally made from fossil fuel feedstocks such as oil and natural gas. Because our patented technology enables us to use waste plastic as the feedstock, we are reducing reliance on fossil fuels.
